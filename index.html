Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are related to iguanas.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication for chameleons and is also how they can avoid predators.
  • Most chameleons have a tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are very complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts About Camels

  • There are 2 types of camels: Bactrian and Dromedary camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  1. There are around 3,000 cicada species, so they vary in size from 0.75 to 2.25 in (2.2 to 5.5 cm) long.
  2. Cicadas can be black, brown or green and can have red, white or blue eyes.
  3. Their wings are transparent and can seem rainbow-hued when held up to a light source.

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • They have great camouflage for their environment.
  • Their eyes are different to normal bears.
  • Cubs are well protected in their first month.

Interesting Facts About Teddys

  1. The term bear-hug was first recorded in 1846.
  2. The Teddy Bears' Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step.
  3. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
